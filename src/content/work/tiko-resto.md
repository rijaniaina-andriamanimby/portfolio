**Tiko’Resto** is a web-based restaurant management application that helps staff manage daily operations more efficiently.  
The platform supports **order tracking, payments processing, menu management, and revenue statistics**, allowing restaurant teams to monitor activity in real time and improve service workflow.

The system centralizes operational data, reduces manual tasks, and provides better visibility into business performance through automated dashboards and generated reports.
